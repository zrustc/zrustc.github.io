Zhirui Zhang(张志锐)

 

Algorithm Expert at Alibaba Group

I am currently an Algorithm Expert in the Language Technology Lab at Alibaba DAMO Academy. Prior to joining Alibaba Group, I completed my Ph.D. in the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Also, I was in a joint Ph.D. program between USTC and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) under the supervision of Prof. Enhong Chen from USTC and Prof. Harry Shum from MSRA. In addition, I obtained my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, USTC in June 2014.

Research Interests:
Neural Machine Translation, Neural Language Generation, Dependency Parsing, Generative Adversarial Network, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Transfer Learning
